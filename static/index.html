Habitica week at a glance

Habitica week at a glance is a tool that displays your Dailies and ToDo's from Habitica in a week overview calendar format.
The first thing you should do is go to the settings page and put in your HabitRPG user token and key.
This was created for personal use, so it's probably buggy and insecure. Use at your own risk.
